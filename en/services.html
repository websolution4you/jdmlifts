Our Services

Comprehensive portfolio of services in the field of elevator assembly, commissioning, and maintenance for international partners.

Mechanical Assembly

Precise installation of lift car frames, guide rails, cabins, counterweights, and elevator drive units.

Electrical Connection & Start-up

Professional wiring of the elevator shaft, control cabinet, safety switches, and sensors.

Regular Maintenance

Preventive service inspections and maintenance to ensure maximum smoothness and lifetime of the elevator.

Commissioning & Handover

Configuration of Schindler elevator control systems, frequency inverter calibration, and final load tests.

Elevator Modernization

Complete reconstruction and replacement of mechanical and electrical components of older elevators with modern technology.

Schindler Technical Support

Rapid troubleshooting and routine maintenance as a guarantee of reliability for our main contract partner.
