We are experts in
elevator systems

Company JDMlifts s.r.o. specializes in professional assembly of elevators. Our primary activity is long-term cooperation with the multinational leader Schindler, for whom we install and assemble elevator systems in the demanding and dynamic environment of the Netherlands.

Thanks to years of experience, precise work, and an emphasis on the highest safety standards, we can successfully execute even the most complex projects. We handle the complete process from mechanical assembly of the structure to final electrical connection, commissioning, and handover for operation.
